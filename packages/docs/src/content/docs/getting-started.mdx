Vivd is organized around a few simple concepts:

- **Organization**: the team or client space that owns projects.
- **Project**: one website workspace with versions, assets, plugins, and publish settings.
- **Studio**: the editing environment where you refine the site with AI and direct edits.
- **Live site**: the published result on your real domain.

## Fastest path to a first live site

### Prerequisites

- Access to a Vivd workspace.
- A website URL to import, or a clear business description and brand assets.
- A domain you want to publish to later.

### Steps

1. Open Vivd and create a new project.
2. Choose either URL import or the scratch flow.
3. Let Vivd generate the first version.
4. Open the project in Studio and make the first round of refinements.
5. Check your publish settings and finish the pre-publish checklist.
6. Publish to your target domain.

### Expected result

You end with a working draft in Studio and a clear path to a live domain without leaving the platform.

## Choose the right starting point

- Use [Import an Existing Website](/import-existing-website/) when you already have a site and want a faster first draft.
- Use [Create from Scratch](/create-from-scratch/) when you are starting from business information, reference assets, and a desired direction.

## What to do next

- Learn how to iterate faster in [Edit in Studio](/edit-in-studio/).
- Read [Publish Your Site](/publish-your-site/) before connecting the final domain.
